About This Project

Group branding project for Rimbrew, a coffee shop. Personally designed the business card and vehicle (van) wrap, ensuring visual consistency with the overall brand identity created by the group.

The project involved creating a full cafe branding package with menu design, signage, and promotional materials for a modern coffee shop. The design focused on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere that would appeal to coffee enthusiasts.
